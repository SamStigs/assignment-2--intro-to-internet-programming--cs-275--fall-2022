Sleep drugs you can buy without a prescription aren't much safer than Rx drugs. In fact, it's easy to take high doses of them because their active ingredients - the antihistamines diphenhydramine and hoxylamine - aren't only in OTC sleep products such as Sominex and ZzzQuil. They're also in nighttime pain relievers such as Advic PM and Tylenol PM, as well as allergy meds such as Benadryl.
